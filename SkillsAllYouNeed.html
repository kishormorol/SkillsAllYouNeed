Skills
All You Need.

We present a comprehensive directory of every first‑party skill, capability, tool, and extension currently shipping across six major AI assistant ecosystems: Claude, Claude Code, ChatGPT, OpenAI Codex, Gemini, and DeepSeek, including their first‑party developer APIs. This directory now catalogs 38 distinct capabilities, classifies them along nine functional axes, and provides a side‑by‑side results table comparing what each ecosystem can and cannot do. The directory is searchable, filterable, deep‑linkable, and intended as a practitioner's reference rather than an exhaustive academic survey.

§ IV · References & Method

Bibliography & methodology.

n = 12 sources
last verified
20 May 2026

    Methodology. Entries were compiled from official product documentation, changelogs, and first‑party announcements between January 2024 and May 2026. Each capability was verified against its vendor's currently‑published documentation within seven days of the listed revision date.

    A capability is marked Stable when it is generally available to non‑enterprise users without waitlist. Beta indicates limited rollout, opt‑in, or vendor‑labelled preview. Deprecated indicates a capability that has been formally retired but remains accessible to grandfathered users.

    “The directory is a living document. Submissions and corrections are welcome via the source repository.”
